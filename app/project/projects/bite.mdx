## About Bite

Bite is a recipe management app that solves a simple problem: losing recipes across Instagram saved posts, TikTok bookmarks, screenshots, and random websites. Instead of scattered recipes everywhere, Bite gives you one organized place for all your cooking inspirations.

## The Problem

As someone who cooks everyday and love trying new recipes online, I found myself constantly:

- Saving recipes to Instagram save posts only to scroll endlessly trying to find them
- Taking screenshots that get lost in my camera roll
- Bookmarking recipe sites I forget about
- Copying recipe URLs into random notes apps

There had to be a better way.

## The Solution

Bite lets you save recipes from anywhere using multiple methods:

1. **Screenshot/Photo import** - Upload recipe photos, AI extracts the content
2. **URL import** - Paste any recipe link from blogs or social media
3. **Text paste** - Copy/paste recipe text, AI structures it
4. **Manual entry** - Traditional recipe creation for family recipes

## v1 Features (Shipped)

### Core Features

- **<u>User authentication</u>**: Email and Google sign-in
- **<u>Recipe creation</u>**: Manual entry with ingredients and instructions
- **<u>Recipe collection</u>**: View all your saved recipes
- **<u>Recipe details</u>**: Full ingredient and instruction display
- **<u>Protected routes</u>**: Secure, user-specific data

### What's Coming Next

- Recipe editing and deletion
- Image uploads (Cloudinary)
- AI recipe parsing from Instagram/blog links
- Recipe tagging and search
- Meal planning calendar
- Grocery list generation
- Nutrition tracking (macros)

### What's NOT in v1

- Mobile native apps
- Social features / recipe sharing
- Recipe ratings/reviews
- Multiple user accounts/family sharing
